The term Big data and data science is a new buzz word and it is almost used everywhere. Data has grown exponentially in a past decade due to the growth of socialmedia, e-commerce and web applications. Need of the hour is not just to limit the data within the structure, but also ability and flexibility to read and store data from all sources and types, with or without structure. Data scientists use these data to analyze, interpret and create visualizations to understand the data, build models and algorithm using the data and communicate insights. Data science is employs technique from many fields within the broad areas of mathematics, statistics, information science and computer science.
